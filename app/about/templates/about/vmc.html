Vision Statement

  • To be the premier soccer club in Eastern NC
  • Provide youth talent the ability to compete at the highest level
  • Build a reputation of inclusion and diversity
  • Establish a sustainable business with year over year growth
  • Establish a multi-use stadium to drive economic impact for the city of Greenville

Mission Statement

Greenville United is an organization that serves the diverse community of Greenville NC through the beautiful game of soccer. The club inspires to provide a platform for the community to support and help local players showcase their talent on a regional and national level. We are a club committed to bringing our community and local businesses together through the beautiful game of soccer.
